Support & FAQ

How is the AI price calculated? expand_more

EcoPlate weighs: (1) Time urgency — fewer hours = steeper discount, (2) Inventory pressure — more portions = higher urgency signal, (3) Perishability risk — Dairy/Cooked get +5-12% multiplier. Final price includes +-4% variance to model real-time demand.

What does Publish & Broadcast do? expand_more

Adds the deal to the live consumer feed at localhost:5000, copies broadcast text to clipboard, and updates the impact stats. Consumers can then claim the deal.

What is the AI Rewrite button? expand_more

Sends your item details to GPT-4o-mini which generates a more compelling, creative, human-sounding broadcast message — replacing the default template copy.

Can I delete a published deal? expand_more

Yes — go to Inventory and click Delete next to any sale. It is removed from the live feed immediately.
